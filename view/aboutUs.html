About Us

Practicourse is an online education platform that helps educators share videos with students around the world. Using AI, we provide automatic subtitle translations in multiple languages, making it easier for everyone to learn, no matter where they're from.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make quality education accessible to everyone by offering videos with subtitles in many languages. We believe that language should never be a barrier to learning, and we use advanced AI technology to make this possible.

Join Our Community

Whether you're a student looking for practical lessons or an educator wanting to share your knowledge, Practicourse welcomes you. Join our growing community to learn, teach, and grow together!
